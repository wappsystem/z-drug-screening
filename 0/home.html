Targeting Sleep Spindles to Improve Cognition in Ageing

Research Study



Welcome to the Targeting Sleep Spindles to Improve Cognition in Ageing Research Study
We invite you to participate in an exciting new research study investigating the effects of a common sleeping medication on learning and memory in older adults.

Eligibility
You may be eligible to participate in this study if you are:
• Aged 50-75 years old
• Fluent in English


If eligible, you will need to come to the Woolcock Institute of Medical Research in Glebe, Sydney on three occasions. Once for a short daytime screening visit, and twice for overnight sleep studies – each visit separated by at least one week. During the overnight sleep studies, we will measure aspects of your sleep using novel sleep imaging technology and memory before and after sleep. You will receive reimbursement for your time.

Your participation in this study is voluntary.

If you are not eligible, your data will not be used.
